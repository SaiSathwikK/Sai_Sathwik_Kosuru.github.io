Description

The Special AI/ML Lab is dedicated to advancing research in artificial intelligence and machine learning, with a focus on cutting-edge Deep Learning architectures. Our mission is to support innovative projects and foster a collaborative environment for students and researchers.

One of our notable projects involves smart traffic management at Patny X roads, Hyderabad, Telangana, India. This project aims to optimize traffic flow and reduce congestion through intelligent data analysis and real-time decision-making.

The lab has also achieved significant milestones with 9+ research publications in reputed journals and conferences, contributing valuable knowledge to the AI/ML community.

We are currently working on a patent under process focused on architecture-performance correlations, which seeks to understand and enhance the efficiency of AI/ML models.

In addition to these projects, the lab has conducted research on hydroclimatological structures, exploring the application of AI/ML techniques to environmental and climate-related challenges.
