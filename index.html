Has AI reached the pinnacle of Human Cognition? How advanced are its current knowledge acquisition capabilities? These questions sparked my interest in research on Epistemology in AI, calling it Epistemic AI.

Epistemology is a philosophical theory that deals with human knowledge acquisition, reception, and reasoning. Due to the omnipresence of data-driven solutions in recent technologies, I find it interesting to integrate epistemology in AI.

I primarily researched on 3 kinds of knowledge: Factual, Clinical, and Knowledge Adaptations.

Throughout my undergraduate studies, I collaborated with multiple Government Officials and R&D Departments through the communities I founded such as Student Research Cell, WeHub, Special AIML Research Lab, etc.

My extensive research activities resulted in 9 research papers, 3 patents, 3 research communities, 3 inter-institutional collaborations, 3 internships/fellowships/assistantships and 5 event-based contributions.

I look forward to continuing my research on the question: "How can the architectural-performance trade-offs be transformed into supplementary knowledge channels for AI to self-assess itself?".
