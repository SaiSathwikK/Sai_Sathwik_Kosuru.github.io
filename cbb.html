Coding Brigade BVRIT

Main Vision: Encouraging coding activities in students

My Role: Head of Technical Development

The Coding Brigade BVRIT is a student-driven club dedicated to encouraging coding activities among students. Our mission is to provide career support, enhance skill development, and improve knowledge on various tech stacks.

We organize numerous career events and hackathons to foster an engaging and competitive environment. With over 6 career events and 7 hackathons, we provide ample opportunities for students to showcase their talents and learn from peers.

Our club also emphasizes the importance of research, resulting in 2 publications that contribute to the academic community.

Activities

  • Career Support: Providing guidance and resources to help students achieve their career goals.
  • Skill Development: Offering training sessions and workshops to enhance coding skills.
  • Tech Stack Improvement: Educating members on the latest technologies and tools.
  • Career Events: Organizing over 6 events focused on career development and networking.
  • Hackathons: Hosting more than 7 hackathons to promote innovation and problem-solving skills.
  • Publications: Encouraging research and contributing to the academic community with 2 publications.
